The stability ball exercises gets a bad rap. Gym bros look at it and ponder how many gains can be had with what looks like a beach ball. Some women look at it and think, “I can do abs on that.” Many fitness professionals don’t like it, and some have even attacked it with a screwdriver (true story). There’s a lot of love or hate for the stability ball, but when programmed and used sensibly, the stability ball is a tool you need in your toolbox. The stability ball will help you get stronger and more resilient against injury, and you’ll have a ball. Sorry, not sorry.
